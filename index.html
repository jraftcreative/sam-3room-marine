Room 01 · Kitchen
The Kitchen
Two takes on Modern Luxury — a bright, airy kitchen in light oak and marble, or a moody, dramatic one in walnut and dark stone. Same clean, handle-less language; two very different feels.
Two Directions
  • Light — oak veneer, white marble-look stone, slim black tapware
  • Dark — walnut veneer, charcoal stone, black hood & fittings
  • Shared — handle-less fronts, full-height stone backsplash, integrated appliances
  • 600×600 floor tiles, under-cabinet lighting

Living · Bar Counter
Bar Counter
& Cabinet
A statement bar anchors the dining zone — a peninsula counter with a lit display cabinet for glassware and bottles. Light or dark, it’s the entertaining centrepiece.
Two Directions
  • Light — cream cabinetry, fluted bar front, brass accents, lit glass display
  • Dark — black marble waterfall counter, walnut fluting, moody pendants
  • Shared — full-height display cabinet, integrated lighting, bar seating
  • Stone / quartz counter with waterfall edge
Room 02 · Living
Feature Wall
& Console
The living hall's centrepiece — a full-height TV feature wall with floating console and lit display. Light marble or dark fluted walnut; both keep the floor open and the room feeling larger.
Two Directions
  • Light — marble feature wall, oak fluting, lit display niches, white console
  • Dark — walnut-fluted wall, charcoal shelving with LED strips, dark console
  • Shared — full-height feature wall, suspended console, integrated lighting
  • Floating display shelves flanking the TV

Room 03 · Master Bedroom
Master
Bedroom
A calm, hotel-like retreat with a full-height wardrobe and a feature headboard wall — light and airy, or deep and moody; both keep sightlines long and the room serene.

Common

A considered guest bath with a glass shower screen and floating vanity — soft and warm, or dark and dramatic; both keep the wet and dry zones clearly defined.
